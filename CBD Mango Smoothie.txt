CBD Mango Smoothie

Published: April 2019

Stripped from the web: 02 May 2019

To give this smoothie some heat, add a pinch of crushed red pepper flakes and a little bit more salt before you blend. We developed this recipe using a 1,000 milligram (mg) bottle of Lily CBD. It's part of the Healthyish Guide to Cooking With, Eating, and Truly Enjoying Weed in collaboration with Broccoli. Click here to read more about how to make cannabis delicious.

2 servings

Ingredients:

1 mango, peeled, chopped

1 banana, chopped

1 1" piece ginger, peeled, finely grated

½ cup unsweetened coconut water

½ tsp. CBD oil

½ tsp. ground turmeric

Pinch of kosher salt

Recipe:

Purée mango, banana, ginger, coconut water, CBD oil, turmeric, salt, and 1 cup ice in a blender until smooth. Divide between glasses.
